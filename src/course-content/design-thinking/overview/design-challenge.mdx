# Design Challenge

## What is our design challenge?

Design an app that improves the quality of life for a group that has experienced negative changes in their life during the pandemic.

## What are our constraints?

There are time and technological constraints to this project:

- You have approximately 3.5 days to work on your app.
- You will be building your app with Expo.
